Solar Day

A clean, offline solar tracking app — built to respect your privacy.

Solar Day helps you understand the sun’s position and daily solar patterns without accounts, ads, or analytics.

Why Solar Day

  • Works entirely offline
  • No sign-ups or accounts
  • No analytics, ads, or tracking SDKs
  • Fast, lightweight, and battery-friendly
  • Designed for clarity and calm

Privacy by Design

Solar Day is built on a simple principle: your data stays on your device — because we don’t collect any.

We believe solar tools should be useful, transparent, and trustworthy. That means no hidden data flows and no behavioral tracking.
